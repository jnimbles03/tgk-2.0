MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

Effective as of May 1, 2025

1. DEFINITIONS

This Master Services Agreement (this "Agreement") is effective as of May 1, 2025 ("Effective Date") and entered into between Acme Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Customer"), and MarketPulse Dynamics Inc. ("Company"). This Agreement governs Customer's use and purchase of the Cloud Offerings and Professional Services (together, "Services") provided by Company to Customer.

"Annual Subscription Fee" means the annual fee applicable to Customer's subscription to a Service, and excludes any one-time fees (e.g., implementation fees, Professional Service fees, and any Taxes defined below).

"Term" means the duration this Agreement is in effect as described in Section 5 below, with an initial term of twelve (12) months and automatic renewal thereafter unless terminated per Section 6.

2. SERVICES

Company will provide the Services described in each Statement of Work. All deliverables are subject to Customer's acceptance in accordance with the acceptance criteria set forth therein.
