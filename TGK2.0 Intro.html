The short version

TGK 2.0 is a demo platform built from reusable blocks. That let the team go from a vertical idea to a usable out-of-the-box flow fast — then keep polishing the story without starting over.

  • Fast vertical spin-up, because the parts are already there.
  • Consistent quality, because the shell and flows are standardized.
  • Custom feel, because the AE can still tune the narrative.

What that means for AEs

You’re not assembling a demo from raw lumber. You’re pulling the right bricks off the shelf and building a story that fits the customer — quickly enough to keep momentum, polished enough to keep attention.

  • Less setup friction.
  • More time for discovery and tailoring.
  • A better-looking demo in less time.
