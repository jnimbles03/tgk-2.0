IAM for Fraud: Scaling Risks Require Scaling Solutions
Docusign IAM provides a spectrum of authentication and identification methods. The right level of verification is applied based on the perceived risk and financial magnitude of each transaction.

Scaling risks require scaling solutions

Let the transaction's risk profile determine the authentication strength — not static rules.

The Docusign IAM Approach to Fraud
Instead of applying the same verification to every transaction, Docusign IAM lets you scale authentication proportionally to risk. A $50 address change gets an access code. A $2M asset transfer gets biometric IDV with liveness detection. And every method — from email to notary — feeds into a single, continuous chain of custody that your fraud and compliance teams can actually defend.
