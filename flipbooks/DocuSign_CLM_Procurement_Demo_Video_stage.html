DRILLING SERVICES MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Drilling Services Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of November 1, 2025 between Black Mesa Energy, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Customer"), and Tritan Drilling Services, Inc., a Texas corporation ("Contractor"), and governs Contractor's provision of land-based drilling services on Customer's Permian Basin properties.

2. SCOPE & DAY RATE

2.1 Rigs. Contractor shall provide and operate five (5) Tier 2 land rigs, each meeting the Schedule A specifications, on Customer's Wolfcamp and Bone Spring leases.

Day rate: $28,400 per rig-day, indexed quarterly to Permian rig count per Schedule B.

2.2 Standby & Move Rates. Standby rate is sixty percent (60%) of day rate. Move rate is forty percent (40%) of day rate or actual cost, whichever is greater.

3. HSE COMPLIANCE

3.1 TRIR Threshold. Contractor's twelve-month rolling Total Recordable Incident Rate ("TRIR") shall not exceed 1.0. Customer may suspend drilling operations and withhold day rates if TRIR exceeds threshold for two consecutive months. Contractor's current TRIR is 0.84 as of the Effective Date.

3.2 ISN Verification. Contractor shall maintain active ISNetworld registration in good standing throughout the Term and shall refresh HSE attestations no less frequently than every thirty (30) days. Customer shall have the right to audit Contractor's HSE program upon thirty (30) days' notice.

3.3 Stop-Work Authority. Both parties grant their personnel unconditional authority to halt operations upon perception of imminent risk to person, property, or the environment, without retaliation or wage forfeiture.

4. INSURANCE & INDEMNIFICATION

4.1 Coverage Minimums. Contractor shall maintain (i) General Liability insurance of not less than $25M per occurrence; (ii) Auto Liability of $5M; (iii) Workers' Compensation as required by law; and (iv) Operator's Extra Expense (control of well) of $50M. Certificate of Insurance shall name Black Mesa Energy as additional insured.

4.2 Indemnification — Knock-for-Knock. Each party shall defend, indemnify, and hold the other harmless against claims arising out of injury to its own personnel or damage to its own property, regardless of fault, except where such injury or damage is caused by the gross negligence or willful misconduct of the indemnitee. Indemnification shall not apply to fines or penalties imposed by regulatory authorities for the indemnitor's violations.

4.3 Force Majeure. Neither party shall be liable for delay or failure in performance caused by acts of God, governmental orders, blowouts, or industrial action affecting the offshore drilling industry generally, provided that the affected party gives prompt notice and uses commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate.
